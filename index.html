Salam arisaye man midooni cheghadr delam barat tang shode bood? Haghighatesh dige nemitoonestam tahamol konam ke nabinamet kholase inke khastambegam cheghadr emrooz khoshgel shode boodi joda az hame ina ghesmate bade dastan ine ke man natoonestam biam yani fekr kardam to nemikhai mano bebini be har hal man az door daram mibinamet alan kheili khoshgel shodi mesle hamishe inam ye bakhshe kuchiki az kadoe tavalodet bood ke gharar bood on ruz barat biaram ke fahmidam nisti nashod manam goftam dige zang ina nazanam ruzeto kharab nakonam hosele in harfae maskhararo nadaram asan be manam nrmiad injoori basham kole dastan ine ke khastam ghable inke beram tehran bebinamet shayad dige ahare mah vaght ke jvaba omad vaght nashe, koli moraghebe khodet bash eshghe man,kheili dooset daram nimvajabi.
